Home Services Plans FAQ

Comprehensive care for your pet

Connect with veterinarians, walkers and certified caregivers near you. All from an app, with tailored plans.

Our Services

Safe Walks

We offer supervised walks so your pet can enjoy physical activity in a safe and controlled environment.

Veterinary Check-Ups

We perform regular checkups to ensure your pet’s health, with personalized consultations and advice.

Grooming

We care for your pet’s coat with baths, cuts and grooming. Hygiene and well-being come first.

Emergencies

Rapid emergency care, with professionals ready to care for your pet when they need it most.

Plans

VIP Plan

Access to all our exclusive services, such as unlimited walks, priority attention in emergencies and personalized vaccine reminders.

Complete Plan

  • 2 weekly walks
  • 1 grooming session per month
  • 1 home veterinary visit
  • Vaccine and deworming monitoring
  • 24/7 support in the app

Mini Plan

  • 1 weekly walk
  • Vaccine reminders
  • Access to certified suppliers
  • Monthly virtual review with veterinarian

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the walking service work?

Our certified walkers will be available according to your area. Choose the schedule that suits you and enjoy a safe walk for your pet.

Are suppliers certified?

Yes, all veterinarians, walkers, groomers and caregivers are verified and have the necessary experience to offer excellent service.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the app, and you will not be charged the following month.

How can I subscribe to a plan?

You just have to download the app, choose the plan that suits you most and complete the record with your data.

Can I change plan at any time?

Yes, you can change plan when you want it from the app, without additional charges.

What happens if my pet needs emergency care?

With the complete plan and VIP, you have 24/7 support through the app. In addition, you can contact urgent veterinarians if necessary.
